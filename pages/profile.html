About me

Me and what i do

My name is Boaz Vaneveld. I am 20 years old and have been living in Middelburg all my life. I love animals and my house is home to several pets, although not all of them belong to me. I have a couple of hobbies! I have a bit more time for some than others but it's difficult to fit everything you want to do into your life.

  • Archery. I have practiced archery for quite some time, but i unfortunately had to stop. I wasn't the best but i had fun doing it and that's what counts right? My time practicing this sport will always be a fond memory.
  • Badminton. One of my more recent endeavors trying to find a sport that suits me. This was quite a lot of fun! Of course the mandatory warm-ups always had to spoil the fun.
  • Chess. Quite a few years i have been playing chess now, but always only once a month or less. I never got all that good at it but the people around me are even worse, so i still win a lot!
  • Writing. This is hobby i spent the most amount of time on. I hope to one day publish a book of my very own! It sure is difficult though, i have to plan out my entire story before i even start to write it!
  • Drawing. I like to draw when i'm bored, which isn't all that often surprisingly. Still, i like what i put on the pages and it's a good outlet for my creativity.
